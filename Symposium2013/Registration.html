Registration

The online registration is processed via PayPal buttons below (linked with the Rosetta Type Foundry e-shop, you can pay with your card directly). If you wish to pay with a bank transfer, contact us via email. We will issue an invoice in a few days after the payment is processed. You will also receive an email with all necessary information few days before the conference.

In order to get the discounted offer, students will need to show their student ID at the venue.

The price includes VAT. If you are an EU-registered VAT payer, contact us via email (with your VAT ID) and we will issue a proper invoice with VAT discounted.

Accommodation and meals are not included. Workshops are paid for separately. The programme is subject to change without notice. We are not liable for refunds in the event of any unforeseen program changes. Refund is possible only as late as one month before the start of the event (small fee applies).

About TypeTalks

Typefaces and textual information in general, despite their crucial importance to our culture and development, are often neglected when it comes to visual communication and graphic design. This problem is particularly acute in the Central European, post-communist environment. TypeTalks was founded by David Březina as a collaborative activity designed to raise awareness of the power of typeface design and typography among both graphic design professionals and students.
